Privacy Policy for SubTally

Last updated: April 21, 2026

Introduction

SubTally ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

Data Collection

SubTally does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. All your subscription data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

What We Don't Collect

  • Personal identification information
  • Financial account credentials
  • Bank account or credit card details
  • Browsing history or usage analytics
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers

Local Data Storage

All subscription information you enter is stored exclusively on your device. We never have access to your data. This means:

  • Your data stays on your device at all times
  • We cannot see, read, or access your subscription information
  • If you delete the app, your data is permanently removed

Calendar Integration

SubTally offers optional calendar integration. If you choose to enable this feature:

  • We request permission to access your calendar
  • Calendar events are created locally on your device only
  • We do not transmit calendar data externally
  • You can disable this feature at any time in Settings

Notifications

SubTally sends local notifications for subscription reminders. These notifications are processed entirely on your device and do not involve any data transmission.

In-App Purchases

SubTally offers a one-time Pro upgrade purchase. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Data Export

The CSV export feature generates a file locally on your device. We do not have access to exported data.

Children's Privacy

SubTally is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Third-Party Services

SubTally does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
