Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a subscription?
Tap the + button on the Home screen. Fill in the subscription details including name, amount, billing cycle, and next billing date. Tap "Add" to save.
How do I edit or delete a subscription?
Tap on any subscription to view its details. Use the "Edit" button to modify details or the menu (⋯) to delete it.
Does SubTally access my bank account?
No. SubTally is a privacy-first app. We never ask for or access your bank accounts, credit cards, or financial credentials. All data is entered manually and stored locally on your device.
How do calendar reminders work?
When you enable "Sync to Calendar" for a subscription, SubTally creates calendar events for upcoming billing dates. You'll need to grant calendar access permission the first time you enable this feature.
What's included in the free version?
The free version lets you track up to 5 subscriptions with all core features including reminders, analytics, and cancel guides. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited subscriptions and calendar sync.
How do I cancel a subscription?
Go to Settings → Cancel Guides to find step-by-step instructions for cancelling popular services. You can also tap "How to Cancel" from any subscription's detail view.
Is my data safe?
Absolutely. All your data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or have access to any of your information. See our Privacy Policy for details.
How do I export my data?
Tap the menu button (⋯) on the Home screen and select "Export CSV". You can also find this option in Settings. The exported file can be opened in Numbers, Excel, or Google Sheets.
